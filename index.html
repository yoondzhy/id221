[P1] Small Multiples

World Flavors Blended on Kazakh Dastarkhan

This poster illustrates the richness of Kazakh cuisine in its diversity and each nation’s significance on our sacred dastarkhan. There are mainly dishes from various countries deeply woven into our daily eating habits, ones I especially love and miss, as well as some of the irreplaceable and significant dishes of authentic Kazakh cuisine, without which dastarkhan wouldn’t be complete.

[P2] Infographics

Gen Z Trapped: Infinite Scrolls, Clicks, Fatigue

This infographic examines doomscrolling as a behavioral pattern common among Gen Z. Using clear visual hierarchy and data-driven elements, the project communicates how infinite scrolling designs contribute to cognitive fatigue and compulsive media consumption.

[P3] Data Visualization

NCT U Network

This project visualizes collaboration patterns within NCT U by mapping connections between members across different releases. The network structure reveals central figures, frequency of collaboration, and how the group’s flexible unit system operates over time.
